This allows delegation without giving full control.                           Business Value History:           Every update to Left/Right Business Values should create an immutable history record for transparency and auditing.                           Member Status:           Support Active, Inactive, and Archived statuses rather than deleting members, preserving historical data.                           Approval Workflow for Events:           Allow registrations to move through statuses such as             Pending          ,             Approved          ,             Rejected          ,             Cancelled          , and             Attended          .                           Reusable Content Categories:           Organize News, Documents, and Media with tags and categories to improve discoverability as the platform grows.                           Dashboard KPIs:           Add trend indicators (e.g., month-over-month growth in business volume, new members, and event participation) to provide better insights for leadership.
